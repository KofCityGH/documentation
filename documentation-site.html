Construction Progress Overview

Site Preparation
This video shows the initial preparation of the construction site. The land has been cleared and leveled to establish the layout for the future building. At this stage, the terrain is being shaped to support the foundation work and ensure proper drainage and structural stability.
Foundation and Block Work
This stage captures the foundation construction process. Concrete blocks are being placed to form the base structure of the building, defining the layout of rooms and load-bearing walls. This phase is critical for ensuring structural integrity before the walls and upper sections are built.
Structural Elevation and Roofing
This video shows the house at a more advanced stage of construction. The exterior walls have been erected and the roof structure has been installed. The project has moved from foundation work to visible building form, marking significant progress toward completion.
